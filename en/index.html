    What is MetroBinari? #

    MetroBinari is a project that was created with the aim of providing a basic and clear representation of the Metro network of some cities. The goal is to collect in an easy and convenient map to consult various details that usually cannot be found in public transport maps. The maps are in 1:25.000 scale

    The information collected in the various maps are:

    • Railroad switches positions
    • Location of the platforms
    • Opening / closing dates
    • Planimetric route
    • Outdoor sections
    • Vehicle deposits
    • Service tracks
    • Sections under construction
